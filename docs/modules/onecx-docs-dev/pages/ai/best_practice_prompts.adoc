= AI: Best Practice Prompts

This page provides best practices for crafting prompts when working with AI agents in the context of support development. Effective prompts can significantly enhance the quality of responses generated by AI models, leading to more accurate and helpful outputs.

== General Best Practices for Prompting
1. **Be Clear and Specific**: Clearly state your request or question. Avoid ambiguity to ensure the AI understands your intent.
2. **Provide Context**: If your prompt relies on specific information or a particular scenario, include that context in your prompt. This helps the AI generate more relevant responses.
3. **Use Structured Prompts**: When possible, structure your prompts in a way that guides the AI. For example, you can use bullet points, numbered lists, or specific formatting to indicate the desired output format.
4. **Iterate and Refine**: If the initial response is not satisfactory, refine your prompt and try again. Experimenting with different phrasings or additional context can lead to better results.
5. **Set Expectations**: If you have specific requirements for the response (e.g., length, tone, format), include those in your prompt to guide the AI in generating the desired output.
6. **Use Examples**: Providing examples of the desired output can help the AI understand what you are looking for and improve the quality of the response.
7. **Avoid Overloading**: Keep your prompts concise and focused. Overloading the prompt with too much information can lead to confusion and less accurate responses.
8. **Test with Different Models**: If you have access to multiple AI models, test your prompts with different ones to see which model provides the best response for your specific needs.
9. **Be Mindful of Bias**: Be aware of potential biases in AI responses and craft your prompts in a way that minimizes the risk of generating biased or inappropriate content.
10. **Use Follow-Up Prompts**: If the initial response is incomplete or requires further clarification, use follow-up prompts to ask for additional information or to clarify the response.
